Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started on GigCampus?

Simply sign up with your university email address, create your profile, and start offering your services or browse available services from other students.

Is GigCampus safe for students?

Yes! All students are verified with university email addresses and government ID. We also have a robust rating and review system to ensure quality and safety.

How do payments work?

We support UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. Payments are held in escrow until service completion, ensuring both parties are protected.

Can I offer services from my home?

Absolutely! Many services can be provided online or from your location. You can specify your preferred meeting method in your profile.

What if I have a problem with a service?

Our support team is here to help! Contact us through the platform or email support@gigcampus.in, and we'll resolve any issues quickly.

Do you charge any fees?

We charge a small platform fee (5%) only when you earn money. There are no upfront costs or hidden fees for using GigCampus.
