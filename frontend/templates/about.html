About GigCampus

Connecting students across India to create opportunities and build communities

Our Story

GigCampus was born from a simple idea: what if students could help each other while earning money? Founded in 2024 by a group of university students in Mumbai, we recognized that students have incredible skills and knowledge that could benefit their peers.

Today, we're India's leading student marketplace, connecting over 50,000 students across 200+ universities. From IITs and NITs to private universities, we're building a community where students can learn, earn, and grow together.

Our Mission

To empower students by creating a safe, reliable platform where they can offer their skills, learn from peers, and build meaningful connections that last beyond graduation.

Our Vision

To become India's largest student community where every student can find opportunities to grow, learn, and earn while contributing to their peers' success.

Our Values

Trust, transparency, and community. We believe in creating an environment where students can thrive, learn from each other, and build lasting professional relationships.
